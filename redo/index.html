Welcome to Chem’s Agro & Marketing Ltd

We provide high-quality agricultural solutions to farmers across Zambia.

Introduction

Chem’s Agro & Marketing Ltd is a leading provider of agricultural solutions in Zambia. Since our establishment in 2001, we have been committed to empowering farmers with high-quality inputs, innovative farming techniques, and expert guidance. Our company operates in 8 districts, ensuring that small-scale and commercial farmers have access to essential farming supplies at competitive prices.

We believe in sustainable farming practices that not only increase productivity but also protect the environment for future generations. Our services include agro-chemical distribution, financial support, farm equipment sales, and value- added agro-processing. We are dedicated to bridging the gap between farmers and markets by providing reliable output aggregation and efficient logistics.

At Chem’s Agro & Marketing Ltd, our goal is to be the most trusted agro-service provider in Zambia. We strive to improve the livelihoods of farmers by delivering affordable and quality agricultural products. Join us in our mission to revolutionize agriculture and create a prosperous farming community.

About Us

We started as a small agro-shop in 2001 and have grown into a leading agricultural supplier across 8 districts in Zambia. Our commitment is to provide farmers with innovative and affordable agro-solutions. Our services include smart agriculture, financial assistance, and value addition.

Meet Our Founder & Manager

Mr. Enernest Chikote founded Chem’s Agro & Marketing Ltd in 2001 with a vision to revolutionize farming in Zambia. With over 20 years of experience, he has expanded the company to 8 districts, providing quality agricultural products and services to farmers. His leadership continues to drive the company towards sustainability and innovation.
